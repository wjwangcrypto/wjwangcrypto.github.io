About me

Weijia Wang was born in Xuzhou, China in 1988. He received PhD degree from the Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2018, supervised by Prof. Yu Yu and Prof. Dawu Gu. He was a Postdoc researcher at Université catholique de Louvain, Crypto Group (hosted by Prof. F.-X. Standaert). Since 2014, he is a Professor at Shandong University, China in 2020.

Research

His research interests include:

  • hardware security

  • cryptographic engineering

  • side-channel attacks & countermeasures
